Second Dawn Combat Calculator

Description

Eclipse: Second Dawn for the Galaxy players wager victory or defeat on the toss of the die. Sometimes, it's hard to tell whether engaging in a fight is a sound strategy, or sure defeat. This app is designed to help players determine the odds of success.

In order to use this app, you will need to add the ships both fleets are using (Presets are available). You may then adjust the total combat statistics of each ship type to reflect your fleet's Upgrade Loadout. If you win the battle, you'll also find out how likely your ships are to have survived.
