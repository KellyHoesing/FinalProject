Second Dawn Combat Calculator

Welcome

In Eclipse: Second Dawn for the Galaxy, players wager victory or defeat at the roll of the dice. This app exists to help them decide whether a particular combat is wise, or folly.

In order to use this app, you simply add the ships involved in the fight to either side, increase or decrease their starting values to match their current values, then press the Calculate button to get your odds of victory.
